About The Book

Mastering Sales and Distribution in FMCG is a comprehensive guide designed for sales professionals, entrepreneurs, and distributors looking to optimize their sales and distribution strategies in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) industry.

This book covers proven techniques for increasing sales, building strong distributor relationships, negotiating better deals, and maximizing profits. It provides actionable insights on modern trade, general trade, pricing tactics, and key account management—all essential for succeeding in the competitive FMCG landscape.

Key Highlights

  • ✔ Key Account Management – How to manage and negotiate with supermarkets, wholesalers, and retailers
  • ✔ Retail Execution Strategies – Ensuring products are well-positioned, stocked, and promoted for maximum sales
  • ✔ Pricing Models for FMCG – Understanding cost-plus, competitive, penetration, and premium pricing strategies
  • ✔ Negotiation Techniques – How to secure better shelf space, price discounts, and long-term contracts
  • ✔ Sales Team Management – How to recruit, train, and motivate high-performing sales teams

Whether you’re a beginner in FMCG sales or an experienced professional, this book equips you with practical strategies to increase revenue and expand your market presence.
